About This Initiative

The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), Taraba State Chapter, is launching this Digital Welfare Registry to enhance member welfare services. As part of the Welfare Secretary's mandate, this platform ensures:

  • Professional management of member data
  • Timely celebration of special occasions
  • Transparent and auditable welfare processes
  • Secure, member-controlled information
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

This platform is officially endorsed by the PSN Taraba State Executive Council.

Privacy Policy

This PSN Welfare Digital Registry complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA).

Data Collection: We collect only necessary information for welfare purposes.

Data Usage: Your information is used exclusively for PSN Taraba welfare activities.

Consent: Registration requires explicit consent for data processing.

Security: Industry-standard encryption protects your data.

Your Rights: You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data.
